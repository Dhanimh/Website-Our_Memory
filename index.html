"Whispers of Love"

In the quiet of the night,
Where stars sprinkle the sky,
I whisper words of love,
That only you and I can hear.

Your laughter, a melody so sweet,
Echoes in the chambers of my heart,
And in your eyes, I find solace,
In every glance, a universe unfolds.

With every touch, a gentle breeze,
Sweeps away the shadows of doubt,
And in your embrace, I find home,
Where love blooms eternal.

Together, we dance in moonlit dreams,
Where time stands still, and worries fade,
For in your love, I am complete,
Bound forever, in this sweet serenade.

So let us write our story,
In pages woven with love and grace,
For you are my forever,
In this world and every place.
